## 1. PURPOSE AND SCOPE

### 1.1 Purpose

This procedure establishes requirements for ensuring personnel involved with AI systems have adequate training and competencies per EU AI Act Article 4 (AI literacy) and Article 14 (human oversight) requirements.

### 1.2 Scope

This procedure applies to:
- AI developers and data scientists
- AI system operators and deployers
- Human oversight personnel
- Management with AI responsibilities
- End users of high-risk AI systems

## 3. TRAINING REQUIREMENTS

### 3.1 Training Categories

| Category | Target Audience | Topics |
|----------|-----------------|--------|
| **AI Awareness** | All employees | AI basics, EU AI Act overview, organizational AI policy |
| **AI Literacy** | AI users and operators | Understanding AI outputs, limitations, appropriate use |
| **Technical AI** | Developers, data scientists | Development practices, compliance requirements |
| **Human Oversight** | Oversight personnel | Monitoring, intervention, override procedures |
| **AI Management** | Managers and executives | Governance, risk, compliance oversight |

### 3.2 Role-Based Training Matrix

| Role | Awareness | Literacy | Technical | Oversight | Management |
|------|-----------|----------|-----------|-----------|------------|
| All Employees | Required | - | - | - | - |
| AI Users | Required | Required | - | - | - |
| Oversight Personnel | Required | Required | - | Required | - |
| Developers | Required | Required | Required | - | - |
| Managers | Required | Required | - | - | Required |
| Executives | Required | - | - | - | Required |

### 3.3 High-Risk AI Additional Requirements

Personnel operating high-risk AI systems must demonstrate:
- Understanding of system capabilities and limitations
- Ability to interpret AI outputs correctly
- Competence to perform human oversight duties
- Knowledge of intervention and override procedures

---

## 4. TRAINING PROCESS

### 4.1 Training Needs Assessment

**Step 1: Identify Training Needs**
1. Review job roles and responsibilities
2. Identify AI systems personnel interact with
3. Assess current competency levels
4. Identify training gaps
5. Document training requirements

**Step 2: Create Training Plan**
1. Assign required training to roles
2. Set completion timelines
3. Schedule training sessions
4. Allocate training resources

### 4.2 Training Delivery

**Training Methods:**
| Method | Use Case |
|--------|----------|
| E-Learning | Awareness, basic literacy |
| Instructor-Led | Technical, oversight |
| Hands-On | System-specific operation |
| On-the-Job | Practical skills development |
| Certification | Specialized competencies |

### 4.3 Competency Assessment

**Step 3: Assess Competency**
1. Administer assessments/tests
2. Evaluate practical demonstrations
3. Review performance on the job
4. Document assessment results
5. Identify remediation needs

**Passing Criteria:**
| Training Level | Minimum Score |
|----------------|---------------|
| Awareness | 70% |
| Literacy | 80% |
| Technical | 85% |
| Oversight | 90% |

### 4.4 Training Records

**Step 4: Maintain Records**
For each employee, maintain:
- Training courses completed
- Completion dates
- Assessment scores
- Certifications obtained
- Refresher training due dates

## 6. TRAINING CONTENT

### 6.1 AI Awareness Training Content

1. What is Artificial Intelligence
2. Types of AI systems
3. EU AI Act overview

5. Organizational AI policy

2. Understanding AI outputs
3. Limitations of AI systems
4. Bias and fairness concepts

2. Monitoring AI system behavior
3. Interpreting outputs and alerts
4. Intervention procedures
5. Override capabilities
6. Documentation requirements
7. Incident reporting
