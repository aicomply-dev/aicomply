## STATUS CODES

- **Open** = Risk identified, treatment pending
- **Treating** = Treatment in progress
- **Monitoring** = Treatment complete, monitoring residual risk
- **Closed** = Risk eliminated or accepted
- **Escalated** = Requires management attention
