**Related Standard:** STD-AI-002: AI Risk Management Standard
**Related Procedure:** PROC-AI-RM-001: AI Risk Management Procedure

---

## Purpose

This form is used to conduct comprehensive risk assessments of AI systems throughout their lifecycle. It supports identification, analysis, evaluation, and treatment of risks to health, safety, and fundamental rights per EU AI Act requirements.

## Instructions

1. Complete this form at each phase of the AI lifecycle
2. Use the Risk Assessment Checklist (CHK-AI-RM-001) as a companion guide
3. Document all identified risks with supporting evidence
4. Obtain required approvals for risk treatment decisions
5. Retain completed forms for minimum 10 years

## SECTION 4: RISK ANALYSIS

### 4.1 Risk Analysis Matrix

For each identified risk, assess likelihood and impact:

**Likelihood Scale:**
- 5 = Almost Certain (>90% probability)
- 4 = Likely (60-90% probability)
- 3 = Possible (30-60% probability)
- 2 = Unlikely (10-30% probability)
- 1 = Rare (<10% probability)

**Impact Scale:**
- 5 = Catastrophic (Severe harm, fatality, major rights violation, existential threat)
- 4 = Major (Significant harm, hospitalization, substantial rights impact)
- 3 = Moderate (Moderate harm, medical treatment required, noticeable rights impact)
- 2 = Minor (Minor harm, first aid only, limited rights impact)
- 1 = Negligible (No harm, inconvenience only)
