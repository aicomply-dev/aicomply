## Purpose

This form documents the transparency disclosures made for AI systems per EU AI Act requirements. Use this form to plan, document, and verify transparency disclosures to users and affected persons.

## Instructions

1. Complete this form for each AI system requiring transparency measures
2. Document all disclosure mechanisms implemented
3. Attach evidence of disclosures (screenshots, scripts, labels)
4. Review and update upon system changes
5. Retain for 10 years
