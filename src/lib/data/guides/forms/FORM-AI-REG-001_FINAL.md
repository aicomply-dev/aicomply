# FORM-AI-REG-001: EU Database Registration Form

**Form ID:** FORM-AI-REG-001
**Form Name:** EU Database Registration Form
**Version:** 1.0
**Effective Date:** [To be filled]
**Related Standard:** STD-AI-011: AI Registration Standard
**Related Procedure:** PROC-AI-REG-001: Registration Procedure

---

## Purpose

This form captures all information required for registering high-risk AI systems in the EU database per Article 71 of the EU AI Act. Complete this form before placing a high-risk AI system on the Union market or putting it into service.

## Instructions

1. Complete all applicable sections based on your role (Provider or Deployer)
2. Ensure information matches technical documentation
3. Submit to EU database before market placement/deployment
4. Update registration for any substantial modifications
5. Retain copy of registration for 10 years
