## Purpose

This checklist provides a comprehensive guide for conducting conformity assessments of high-risk AI systems per EU AI Act requirements. It ensures all necessary requirements are verified before placing high-risk AI systems on the market or putting them into service.

## Instructions

1. Complete this checklist for all HIGH-RISK AI systems before deployment
2. Mark each requirement as Met, Partially Met, Not Met, or Not Applicable
3. Document evidence and location for each requirement
4. Address all "Not Met" and "Partially Met" items before proceeding
5. Obtain all required approvals before issuing EU Declaration of Conformity
