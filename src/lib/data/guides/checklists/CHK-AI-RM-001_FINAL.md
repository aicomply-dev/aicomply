## Purpose

This checklist provides a systematic guide for conducting AI system risk assessments per the EU AI Act requirements. Use this checklist alongside the Risk Assessment Form (FORM-AI-RM-001) to ensure comprehensive risk identification and assessment.

## Instructions

1. Complete all applicable sections
2. Mark each item as Complete, Partial, Not Applicable (N/A), or Not Started
3. Document evidence and notes for each item
4. Address all gaps before proceeding to next lifecycle phase
5. Review and update at each lifecycle phase
