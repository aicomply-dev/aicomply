## Purpose

This checklist ensures that post-market monitoring (PMM) activities for AI systems are conducted systematically and in compliance with EU AI Act Article 72 requirements. Use this checklist for periodic review of PMM activities.

## Instructions

1. Complete this checklist periodically (monthly for high-risk, quarterly for others)
2. Mark each item as Complete, Partial, Not Done, or Not Applicable
3. Document evidence and findings
4. Escalate any significant issues identified
5. Update PMM plan based on findings
